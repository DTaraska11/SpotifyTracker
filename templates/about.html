How it works:


1. Register an account (any playlist records you make will automatically be saved and accessible from 'My Playlists')

2. 'Create Record'

3. Enter the username of the SPOTIFY account that created the playlist, along with the playlist id.

The id can be found in the url of the playlist.

For example: https://open.spotify.com/playlist/3bqTRt46r28sw4YP92m6Dd

'3bqTRt46r28sw4YP92m6Dd' is the playlist ID

4. Submit. For large playlists, this will take an extremely long time.

5. Once it loads, you'll be presented with a view of the metadata of all the tracks in your playlist.

6. 'My Playlists'

7. 'View Records' for the desired playlist

8. All the records you've created with the associated timestamps

9. Check any two versions and hit 'Compare'
